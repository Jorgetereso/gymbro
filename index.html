The Plan

How To Use This

4 sessions a week, any 4 days that fit around golf (Fri / Sat). Rotate Push → Pull → Legs → Pump & Core. Use Full Body when you can only fit one session that week.

On the TODAY tab pick the session, then log every working set: weight × reps. The next session of the same type will show you what you did last time — your job is to beat one number.

Progressive overload = add a little weight, do one more rep, or one more set. Pick one per week. That's the whole game.

Quick Rules

Warm up. 2-3 light ramp-up sets on the first big lift (Squat / Bench / Row). Then bring intensity.
Last set must be hard. If you finish the last set with 3+ reps in the tank, the weight was too light. Add 2.5-5 kg next time.
Free weights first, machines last. Bar / dumbbell compounds while fresh, machines & cables to pump volume after.
Pull volume ≥ push volume. Most guys are bench-heavy and round forward. Don't be that guy.
